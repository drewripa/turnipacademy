Our Story

Hi, I'm Kateryna

Founder of Turnip Academy

Creating this daycare has been my dream for as long as I can remember.

I grew up in a big family with many younger brothers and sisters. As the oldest sibling, I naturally became the one who cared for them, invented games, planned activities, and found creative ways to make every day fun. Spending time with children never felt like work — it was simply something I loved.

My grandfather used to tell me, "If you find something you truly love, you'll never have to work a day in your life." Those words stayed with me and inspired me to pursue a career in early childhood education.

Children inspire me every day. Their curiosity, imagination, and excitement for discovering the world remind me why I chose this profession. I truly love learning alongside them and helping each child build confidence, independence, and a lifelong love of learning.

At Turnip Academy, my goal is to create more than just a daycare — a place where every child feels safe, loved, and excited to come each morning, and where families feel confident their little one is in caring hands.

The Rhythm of Our Day

From circle-time songs to garden adventures, our days are filled with laughter and learning. We balance energetic play with quiet moments, ensuring a happy and harmonious experience for every child.
